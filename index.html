I

The Architect

Architecture, at its most honest, is memory given structure. I approach design as a careful negotiation between atmosphere and clarity—where proportion, shadow, and material speak with restraint rather than noise.

I am based in Germany, building a practice that places authorship at the center. Each project is treated as a chapter: composed, specific, and intended to hold its presence over time.

This portfolio exists not as a catalogue of services, but as a record of intent—work shaped by discipline, study, and the belief that the built world should feel inevitable.
